# CANVAS QUIZ SCRAPER


Have you ever tried Studying for a Test by going over old quizzes but found yourself rewriting quizzes because you maxed out quiz attempts.
Fear no more, Canvas quiz scraper lets you retake quizzes with the click of a button. Just go to your quiz, click on the the CQS extension,
and choose how you'd like to retake your quiz. You can retake all, mcq, or written questions with the click of a button. 
Studying for tests is already hard enough, let CANVAS QUIZ SCRAPER make studying a little easier.
